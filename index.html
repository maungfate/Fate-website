STUDIO ↘

i.

This is how I worked in my small studio room. I paint on the floor and directly on the canvas without a frame, because of the space limitation I have — which wouldn't allow me to work big strokes and experiment in thick layers. Nevertheless, it turned out to be an opportunity for me to explore mindfully and allowed me to have a closer look at the canvas.

Creativity often grows through limitation.

ii.

The best part is finding the gentle, flowing texture and softness of the acrylic medium — like oil and watercolour. I studied watercolour for three years (2019–22) as my first medium; maybe all the techniques I explored make sense after all.

iii.

This footage was from 2024. I covered up the piece after taking the shot, sensing myself growing attached to it. Detachment felt necessary to achieve the final form and become what it needed to be.
